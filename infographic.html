The Problem

Everything is Manual & Slow

You spend hours manually entering every sale from Flipkart, Amazon, and bulk orders into spreadsheets. This is slow, repetitive, and prone to human error.

The Solution

Automate Your Workflow

The system connects to your sales channels. Orders automatically update your stock. This eliminates nearly all manual data entry, freeing up your team for valuable tasks.

The Problem

Stock Uncertainty & Guesswork

Without a clear, real-time picture of your inventory, it's hard to know what's available. Reordering is based on guesswork, leading to lost sales or tied-up capital.

The Solution

Reorder with Confidence

A live dashboard shows real-time stock levels and sales trends. It automatically alerts you when to reorder, preventing stockouts and overstocking.

The Problem

Resolving Doubts is Difficult

When a student has a query or a supplier sends a partial delivery, you lack a quick, reliable way to verify order details, leading to confusion and potential losses.

The Solution

Gain Complete Visibility

View an end-to-end timeline for every single order. Instantly see the exact stage of any delivery, helping you resolve customer and supplier doubts with accuracy.

The Problem

Reporting is a Manual Chore

Creating daily sales reports and checking closing stock is a time-consuming manual task that delays important business decisions.

The Solution

Get Instant, Accurate Reports

See daily sales, top-selling books, and other key metrics instantly on a live dashboard. Make faster, data-driven decisions for your business with one click.
